Colors

In web development there are hundreds, if not thousands of color pallets to use when making your web application. If I am being honest, to find a lot of these colors you could simply remember your days drawing/coloring in kindergarten,or you could use google (a web developers best friend) and find colors that you would never have imagined to find. Here is three colors that are common use in web development:

  • Red Hex Value: #ff0000
  • Green Hex Value:#008000
  • Blue Hex Value: #0000ff

To find more colors and color pallets, I use the She Codes website. I would highly recommend this to anyone!

Typography

Typography is important in web development. It gives the the developer the ability to make sure their application contains readability for its viewers. Typography, is the art of arranging text on a page. Readability is very important and along with the information provided, visual comfort will keep the viewers focus and attention.

Font-family: There are many fonts to use in your application, some of them are even programmed into your code editor for use. If none of the fonts seemed right for your application you can find more fonts on Google fonts. Here are a few font ideas:

  • Times New Roman
  • Water Brush
  • Verdana
  • Oleo Script Swash Caps

Font-weight:When making your application with different fonts there are ways to make your text appear bold or thin. This would be called the font-weight of your text and is ued to emphasize your caption and get the point across.
